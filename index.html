About


My name is Zakk and I'm seeking employment in the HVAC/R or related trades.

My entry to the industry of HVAC/R was through my family's air conditioning service business. Taking an interest in the business at a young age, I became involved in the logistical side of things first. At the age of 16 I began to work alongside my father to learn the technical aspects of refrigeration and have been doing it professionally since the age of 18. This puts me at over 5 years of field related experience.

I made the decision to leave the family business in pursuit of expanding my knowledge in ways that couldn't have been possible otherwise. I am looking for the best opportunities that the industry has to offer in the way of gaining new and valuable skills. This way I can ensure the most fulfilling future for myself, doing what I love to do, which is solving complex climate control problems.

I am universally EPA certified, posess my own tools, gauge sets, brazing equipment and reliable transportation. My background and driving record are clean.
